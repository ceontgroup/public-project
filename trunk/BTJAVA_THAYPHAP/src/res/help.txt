How to play Puzzle Game.
+ Click mouse on digit cell than next to dot cell
+ The game is finished as all digit cells are in
order of 12345678 clock-wise or anti-clockwise, with the dot cell in the center
and the 1-cell at the left-top corner
+You can have your choice of game when you win the game
+You can save the game and load it later on from disk
+You can change color of the game's board
+Enjoy yourself !
